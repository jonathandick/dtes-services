Glossary

SRO/SRA Single room occupancy/accommodation. One room, shared toilet and bath.
Studio One room with own bath/shower and kitchen facilities.
Shelter Rate BC Income Assistance shelter allowance (~$500/mo). BC Housing subsidizes the gap.
Income-Based Rent set at ~30% of gross income.
Below-Market Rent below average market rates. Also "affordable" or "low-end market."
Supportive On-site support services and staff. Level varies.
Transitional Supportive housing aiming to move residents to independent living (typically ~2 yrs).
Low-Barrier Substance use and behaviour issues accepted.
SHR BC Housing Supportive Housing Registry — primary referral pathway.
BC Hsg Registry BC Housing Registry — for income-based and subsidized units.
MHSUSH VCH Mental Health & Substance Use Supportive Housing — apply via VCH.
Accessible Units designed for wheelchairs and other mobility aids.
