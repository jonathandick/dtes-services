DTES OD Watch

A real-time overdose tracking tool for frontline workers and peer responders. Log events in seconds, visualise emerging hotspots on a live map, filtered by time window.

  • Live heatmap on real DTES streets
  • 3-tap event logging — no typing needed
  • Substance & outcome tracking
  • Privacy-first — no personal data collected

CTCT – Community Transitional Care Team

Residential acute care for people who use drugs discharged from hospital — IV antibiotics, 24/7 nursing, OAT, and housing secured at every discharge. Active use permitted.

  • History & founding context (PA Hotel, 1991)
  • Current care model & harm reduction principles
  • Referral pathway from St. Paul's & VGH
  • HAH partnership (Nov 2025) & co-located PSS
